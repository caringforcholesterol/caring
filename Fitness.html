Warnings and Safety Concerns

Some things your bag could be destroyed by doing are...
Do not bleach your bag, do not wash or dry clean, do not tumble dry, and do not iron. The maximum the bag can hold is 6kg

Some important ways to take care of your backpack are...
Use a warm cloth to clean the inside and outside becuase if you put it in the wash you risk loosening the stitches and it falling apart at the seams...

Is flamable and when ignighted can release toxic fumes into the air.
